Microsoft Teams Solutions

Empowering your business with seamless communication and enterprise-grade Direct Routing services.

Microsoft Teams Configuration

We provide complete Microsoft Teams setup services tailored to your organization. Our team ensures smooth deployment, user enablement, and policy configuration so your staff can communicate and collaborate efficiently.

From licensing guidance to tenant optimization, we handle the technical setup so you can focus on productivity.

Direct Routing

Connect Microsoft Teams to the Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN) through our Direct Routing solutions. This allows your Teams users to make and receive external calls using a secure and reliable Session Border Controller (SBC).

Our Direct Routing services provide cost-effective voice integration, carrier flexibility, and advanced call routing — enabling your business to maintain control and reduce expenses.

Microsoft Teams + Your Existing PBX

Already have an on-premises PBX system? No problem. We integrate Microsoft Teams with your existing PBX, giving you the best of both worlds — modern collaboration in Teams and your trusted phone system.

This hybrid approach protects your current investment while extending advanced cloud collaboration features to your workforce.
