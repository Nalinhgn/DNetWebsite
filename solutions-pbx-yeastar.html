Complete Unified Communication Platform

Yeastar goes beyond a traditional PBX by integrating all business communications into one unified system. Available as cloud, hardware, or software, the Yeastar Business Phone System delivers seamless connectivity across voice, video meetings, call center operations, omnichannel messaging, and CRM integrations.

DNet Solutions designs and deploys Yeastar systems tailored to your needs — enabling powerful collaboration and communication that fits your business model. With a modern interface and AI-powered features, managing calls, meetings, and chats has never been easier.

Linkus UC Clients — Call, Meet & Chat Anywhere

The Linkus UC Clients put a complete business phone system inside a single app — accessible on desktop, mobile, or web. Users can handle calls, host video meetings, chat with teammates, view call logs, access recordings, and manage contacts — all from one interface.
